About us

We're a physiotherapy practice with a special interest in sports injuries, dedicated to providing high-quality, personalised care. Whether you'e aiming to run your first 5km or compete at a professional level, we're here to help you move better, train smarter, and reach your full potential.

With a genuine passion for both sport and people, our goal is to support you every step of the way—helping you stay active, recover effectively, and perform at your best. You'll find us at Gibbs and Associates in Garsfontein, Pretoria East.

Our Approach

At our practice, we believe that accurately identifying the underlying cause of a patient's symptoms is essential for delivering effective treatment and facilitating a safe and timely return to training or competition.

One of the key methods we use is Mechanical Diagnosis and Therapy (MDT), a reliable and evidence-based method, for both assessment and treatment. MDT not only guides clinical decision-making but also empowers patients through education — enabling them to take an active role in managing their symptoms independently.

Our treatment approach may also involve addressing fascial restrictions and implementing targeted rehabilitation and strength programmes, tailored to each patient's unique needs and goals.

Meet the Team

Karli Conradie (Owner and Physiotherapist)

Karli is exceptionally passionate about the field of Sports Physiotherapy and is dedicated to helping athletes perform at their best. She is a credentialled clinician in Mechanical Diagnosis and Therapy (MDT), certified through the internationally recognised McKenzie Institute International.

Her experience includes working with elite athletes both in clinical settings and at national and international track and field events, giving her valuable insight into high-performance sport and injury management.

At Gibbs and Associates, Karli works closely with her mentor, Karen Gibbs, who has been instrumental in her professional growth. She takes pride in being part of a team dedicated to delivering exceptional physiotherapy care, where every patient is treated with expertise, compassion, and respect.

Outside of her professional life, Karli is happily married to her husband Joe, and they are proud parents to their one-year-old son. She values time with her family and enjoys competing in road running-, trail running- and triathlon events.
