About us

We are a Physiotherapy practice with a special interest in sports injuries. We are committed to providing exceptional care to our patients and we are passionate about helping people reach their full potential with regards to sport and lifestyle goals. We are committed to provide high quality physiotherapy care for the sporting population. We believe that a holistic approach is vital in addressing the underlying cause of symptoms and in unlocking ones full potential, whether that is entering your first 5km race or competing as a professional athlete. based at Gibbs and Associates, in Garsfontein, Pretoria East.

Our Approach

We believe that accurately identifying the cause of a patient's symptoms, is crucial to treat them effectively and assist them in getting back to training or competing as soon as feasible. We enjoy using Mechanical Diagnosis and Therapy (MDT) as a means of effectively treating patients. believes that educating patients through the MDT method empowers them to self- manage their pain or other symptoms.
